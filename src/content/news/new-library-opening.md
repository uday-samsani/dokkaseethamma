  We are thrilled to announce the opening of our new community library! 
  
  This facility will serve as a knowledge hub for students and adults alike, providing access to educational resources, computers, and a quiet study space.
  
  ## Library Features
  - Over 2,000 books across various subjects
  - 10 computer workstations with internet access
  - Daily newspapers and magazines
  - Study rooms for group learning
  - Weekend reading programs for children
  
  The library is open Monday through Saturday, 9 AM to 6 PM.
  
  Thank you to all our donors who made this possible!
